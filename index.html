How we met

Tim and Gabrielle would like to celebrate their wedding with you! Bring your bug spray, because we're going out to the woods. Up in the mountains of the north shore of Vancouver, British Columbia, Timofey Zinin and Gabrielle Eschner are celebrating their relationship and are excited for you to partake in the festivities. We are inviting you merrymake, frolic, drink, and dance with us at the camp.
After five years of telling Tim she didn't want to get married, Gabrielle proposed to Tim on a dock under the stars. They loved the camp atmosphere so much that they decided to have a similar wedding. You can expect s'mores, campfire songs, dancing, a ceremony in the woods, and a lot of fun and laughter.
The venue is a cabin built in 1946, in Capilano River Regional Park. Gabrielle actually went to Girl Guide camp there as a child. It's surrounded by tall cedars and features a fire pit where the ceremony will take place. As such, comfortable footwear is recommended.
Due to the nature of the venue parking space is very limited. If you intend to park at the camp please get in touch with us to make arrangements. In addition the camp has dorms, and if you are interested in staying overnight check back here for details SOON.
